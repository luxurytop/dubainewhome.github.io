Payment Plan

The payment plan for The Source Terraces by Aldar Properties is designed to provide flexibility and convenience for our valued buyers. With a range of options tailored to suit different budgets and preferences, we aim to make owning a home at The Source Terraces accessible to all.

Upon reservation of your apartment at The Source Terraces, a nominal booking fee is required to secure your unit. Following this, our flexible payment plan allows for a series of installment payments to be made over the construction period, ensuring that you can manage your finances effectively.

The payment plan typically consists of several milestone payments tied to specific stages of construction, with the final installment due upon completion and handover of your apartment. This phased approach allows you to spread out the cost of your investment over time, making it more manageable and convenient.
